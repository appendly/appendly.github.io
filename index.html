What is Dromo

How will dromo help you deliver packages ?

Dromo is an online marketplace connecting travelers with people who want to deliver packages. On one side, the platform enables travelers to list their available traveling details and earn extra income in the form of rent. On the other, dromo enables those who want to deliver their packages to request,book a traveler by saving them money and giving them a chance to interact with travelers.

We nade a cloud-based, GPS enabled apps that allows people to interact with peer travelers and book them. We generated PTF (Profile Trust Factor ) to ensure safety and to create a circle of trustability,.
